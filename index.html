Welcome to FrostGuard!

FrostGuard is a wearable smart cryotherapy pad designed to provide safe, consistent, and portable cold therapy for fever relief, muscle pain, and minor sports injuries. Equipped with real-time temperature monitoring, FrostGuard ensures users can safely track the device's surface temperature while in use through a built-in OLED display.

This innovative device uses a thermoelectric cooling (TEC) module paired with an integrated temperature sensor to deliver reliable cooling performance. Its compact, lightweight, and rechargeable design makes it ideal for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and individuals seeking convenient cold therapy solutions anytime, anywhere.

How to Use FrostGuard

  1. Charge the Device
    • Make sure your FrostGuard's battery is fully charged. Plug in the charger and wait until the charging indicator turns blue.
  2. Turn On the Device
    • Press the power button once (1) to turn on the device. The OLED screen will light up and the cooling function will start automatically.
  3. Monitor the Temperature
    • Monitor the OLED display to see the current temperature of the cooling pad. Allow about five minutes for the pad to reach the right cooling level before applying.
  4. Apply the Cooling Pad
    • Gently set the FrostGuard on the area you want to cool â€” like your forehead or a sore muscle. Secure it with the velcro strap so it stays in place.
  5. Use Within Recommended Time
    • Use it for about 15 to 20 minutes per session. While you're at it, keep an eye on the temperature display to make sure it stays comfy.
  6. Turn Off the Device
    • When you're done, press the power button again to turn it off. Let it cool down a bit before storing it away!
Note: Always refer to the Safety Reminders before each use to ensure proper and safe operation.

Safety Reminders

Before Use

  • Make sure the device is fully charged and in good condition before operating.
  • Inspect the cooling pad, wires, and display for any visible damage. Do not use if damaged.
  • Clean and dry the skin area where the device will be applied.

During Use

  • Do not use the device for more than 20 minutes per session to avoid skin irritation or cold burns.
  • Regularly monitor the temperature on the OLED display while using the device.
  • If you feel excessive cold, numbness, or discomfort, turn off the device immediately and remove it from the skin.
  • Keep the device away from water or any liquid to prevent damage and electrical hazards.
  • Avoid placing the cooling pad directly on open wounds or areas with poor circulation.

After Use

  • Turn off the device properly and allow it to cool down before storage.
  • Store the device in a clean, dry, and safe place away from direct sunlight and heat.
  • Keep out of reach of small children when not in use.

Important Reminders

  • For external use only.
  • Not recommended for individuals with severe circulatory issues, frostbite history, or skin sensitivity to cold without medical supervision.
  • Do not attempt to disassemble or modify the device.
  • Use only the provided or compatible charger for recharging.
